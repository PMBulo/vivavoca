The Tools
Built for Every Stage
Seven specialized tools that take you from first draft to defense day
🎓 Defense Room

A Real Panel.
In Your Browser.

Dr. VoceAI is a multi-examiner AI panel that speaks out loud, waits for your voice response, then probes, challenges, or escalates based on what you said. Every session is different. Every question adapts to your answer.

  • Up to 3 examiners — each with a distinct voice, name, and questioning style
  • 8 discipline modes from Business to Health Sciences
  • 4 languages: English, French, Portuguese, Swahili
  • Real-time coaching after every single answer
  • Live confidence meter tracking your hesitation and depth
📖 Research Consultant

Understand Every Word
You Wrote.

Dr. WeekB4 reads your dissertation and explains it back to you — chapter by chapter — in language you can confidently repeat in front of an examination panel. It will not move on until you confirm you understand.

  • Upload your PDF and get personalized chapter explanations
  • Choose your explanation style: plain, academic, or defense-ready
  • Ask any question verbally or by typing
  • Progress tracker shows chapters understood vs remaining
  • Speaks explanations aloud so you absorb them naturally
🔍 Dissertation Grader

Know Your Grade
Before You Submit.

Upload your dissertation and receive the same grading an actual examiner would give — complete with rubric breakdown, a letter grade, and an automatic literature gap analysis that flags exactly what needs fixing.

  • Full rubric scoring across 6 assessment dimensions
  • Automatic literature gap detector — citation recency, theory linkage
  • Grade with score mode OR comments-only supervisor mode
  • Download the full grading report as a formatted PDF
  • Grading history saved for comparison over time
